1) Get the service:
   kubectl get svc -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} {{ include "serviceexample.fullname" . }}

2) Port-forward (no ingress):
   kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "serviceexample.fullname" . }} 9080:{{ .Values.service.port }}
   Open http://localhost:9080/swagger/index.html
